Hey you! Thank you for your interest in my work. I am Georgia, a german ux designer and devops enthusiast focused on designing and developing meaningful intuitive digital experiences. At the moment I'm spending all my time in Figma, creating prototypes for a mobile app I can't wait to share with the (german-speaking) world. Before that I work as an IT Consultant spezialiced in DevOps. Previously, I received my masters degree in human-centered computing and my bachelors degree in media and communications informatics. I also do pro bono website design work for causes I believe in, like I did here -> https://www.rumaenienhilfe-baiersbronn.de/. My portfolio is available upon request.
